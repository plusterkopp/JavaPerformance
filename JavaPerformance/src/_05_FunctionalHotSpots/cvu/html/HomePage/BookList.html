BOOK RECOMMENDATIONS

I maintain a book list for use of my readers, customers, attendants of my courses, and everybody else who is interested. It is organized as an annotated reference with a brief description of each title.

The list does not claim to be complete in any way.  A title is included in this list if and only if I personally find it worth the money.

  • Either I read it myself cover to cover and found it useful for my work, or
  • I reviewed it to see whether it would be  a good companion for any of the seminars I teach, or
  • it was recommended to me by a peer who found it useful.
