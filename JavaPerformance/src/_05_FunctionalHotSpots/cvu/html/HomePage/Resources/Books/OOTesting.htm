Testing Object-Oriented Systems: Models, Patterns, and Tools
Robert V. Binder
Addison-Wesley, October 1999
ISBN: 0201809389
To the best of my knowledge this is the authoratative book on testing of object-oriented software.  It introduces fundamental techniques to analyse the class design and derive appropriate tests for its behaviour. Be warned: it comes at the intimidating volume of almost 1000 pages.

The author provides a bibliography of publications related to OO-testing at http://www.rbsc.com/pages/onlineix.html.
